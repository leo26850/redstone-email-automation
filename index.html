Lead-Form Auto-Reply Automation

v3 — Built, dark-launched, pending approval Prepared by KAMG · updated 2026-07-15 Client draft due: Fri 2026-07-17

In one sentence: the instant an enterprise-domain lead submits the quote form, the website itself sends Manuel's personal reply — from his address, with Eric and Alec CC'd, the submission echoed, a booking link included, and the email logged in HubSpot — while free-mail and article-form submissions are silently skipped.

v3 pivot (07/15): Kasandz has no credentials for the sales@ or Manuel mailboxes, so the Apps Script (v2) cannot be deployed by us — it survives as a client-deployable handoff option (BUILD.md). The auto-reply is instead built into redstone-web's own lead pipeline and is already on a PR, deployed dark behind an env flag. This removes the mailbox dependency, the 5-minute delay, and the email-parsing layer entirely — the form handler has the raw data in hand. History: §8.

1The brief

From Eric's email of 2026-07-09. Eight requirements:

  1. Qualify the lead — auto-reply only for enterprise domains (no gmail etc.)
  2. Reply from Manuel's email account
  3. Manuel's exact template/wording from the original lead thread, with Alec's calendar link added at the end
  4. CC Eric and Alec on the auto-reply
  5. Replies that go only to Manuel auto-forward to Alec (if the lead doesn't reply-all)
  6. Email logged in HubSpot for the lead
  7. Include the lead-form submission in the email, like Manuel did
  8. Subject line: "XXXX with Redstone Manufacturing", where XXXX = the manufacturing process from the form

2Architecture (v3 — in-site)

The auto-reply lives inside the website's quote endpoint (/api/quote in kasandzz/redstone-web), running right after the lead is captured. It sends through the site's existing verified Mailgun domain as Manuel Ayala <manuel@redstonemanufacturing.com> with Reply-To his real inbox.

  • No mailbox access needed — the only approach Kasandz can build, test, and deploy end-to-end alone
  • No parsing layer — the handler holds the raw form data and the HubSpot contact id from the upsert it just performed
  • Instant — the reply lands seconds after submission, not on a 5-minute poll
  • Dark launch — code is deployed but sends nothing until the Vercel env var AUTOREPLY_ENABLED=true is set (post-approval)
  • Fail-safe — the auto-reply call never throws; a failure can never affect lead capture, HubSpot, or the sales notification

3System flow

Quote form submission ──► /api/quote (Vercel)
   ├── 1. Validate + honeypot                    (existing)
   ├── 2. HubSpot contact upsert → contactId     (existing)

   ├── 4. Slack alert                            (existing)
   └── 5. sendAutoReply()                        ★ NEW — src/lib/autoreply.ts
          │
          ├─ AUTOREPLY_ENABLED !== 'true'? → skip (dark launch)
          ├─ No process field (article form)? → skip (policy, pending Eric)
          ├─ Free-mail domain?              → skip — no reply ever
          └─ Send via Mailgun:
               From:     Manuel Ayala <manuel@redstonemanufacturing.com>
               Reply-To: manuel@  (lead replies go to his real inbox)
               To:       lead's work email
               CC:       eric@ + alec@redstonemanufacturing.com
               Subject:  "{Process} with Redstone Manufacturing"
               Body:     Manuel's verbatim template + calendar link
                         + signature + submission echoed
             then log an email engagement on the HubSpot contact
             (id already in hand from step 2)
